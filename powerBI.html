SuperTech Retail Project

SuperTech Retail store is a thriving retail chain with multiple stores spanning across diverse regions. They want us to help them track their key business metrics across the business. From the analysis, we were able to help them discover their top-selling product in terms of quantity sold, The customer that made the highest purchase, The total revenue generated by each product category, The city that has the highest sales volume, and how their sales performance varies over time. We gave them suggestions on how to drive their marketing campaign for the product and city that is making the least sales to increase their revenue in the coming year.
